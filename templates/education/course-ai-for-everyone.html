Key Learnings

  • Learned the meaning of common AI terminology: machine learning, deep learning, data science, and neural networks.

Why it matters: Strong AI systems need strong product thinking and organizational alignment. This course strengthened my ability to communicate AI concepts clearly, evaluate feasibility, and connect AI solutions to real-world value.
